Polymer particle technology

The electronics industry’s preferred designer of microparticles.  Extremely narrow size distribution Conpart can utilize a wide range of chemistry, producing polymer particles with unique properties tailored specifically for each application. This unique technology makes it possible to choose from a wide range of monomers, tuning mechanical properties, chemical stability and surface properties, at the same time maintaining spheres with unmatched size distribution and reproducibility. This makes Conpart’s particle technology state-of-the-art within electronics field.
