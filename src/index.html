Particle Technology

The electronics industry’s preferred designer of microparticles.
Extremely narrow size distribution with Confine™ Conpart can utilize a wide range of chemistry, producing polymer spheres with unique properties tailored specifically for each application. This unique technology makes it possible to choose from a wide range of monomers, add inorganic materials to the core and add suitable surface layers, at the same time maintaining spheres with unmatched size distribution and reproductibility which makes Conpart’s particle technology state-of-the-art within the electronics field.
Conparts’ technology enables mono-sized particles with en extremely narrow size distribution compared to conventional methods.
