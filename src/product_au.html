SphericaTM Au & Ni series

Conductive Particles in Anisotropic Conductive Adhesives(ACA)

Monosized gold-coated polymer particles

Spherica™ Au

Spherica™ Au & Ni conductive particles are used in Anisotropic Conductive Films (ACF) and Anisotropic Conductive Paste(ACP) applications which are the major conductive materials used for fine-pitch interconnections in electronic packaging. Spherica™ Au, Ni products can be selected from a variety of particle size, concentration, and capture rate to meet specific applications or requirements.

Product features

  • Truly spherical particles with highly uniformed size.
  • Available particle size is from 3μm to 40μm.
  • Metal thickness can be adjusted for applications.

ACF working principle For Spherica™ Au and Ni product series

  • Spherica™ Au-series: These are nickel&gold coated particles, and the application is for anisotropic conductive adhesive (ACF or ACP)

Applications

  • Electrically Conductive Adhesives for die attach and microelectronics packaging Cost effective silver fillers for Electrically
  • Conductive Adhesives for solar module manufacturing
