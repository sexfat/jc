Our Mission

Conpart's mission is to provide sustainable solutions with high-quality products and services for electronics and solar industries based on our unique particle technology. We create innovation value and opportunity for our customers, employees, investors, and global partners.

Waht we do

We utilize unique particle technologies to develop high-performance inter-connected materials for a wide range of applications including Electronics, Solar, Mobility, etc.
Conductive Particles
Spherica® particles replace expensive pure silver and high density metal powders used in consumer electronics and solar industrial market. Spherica® particles provide lower cost and lower weight reliable solutions to our customers.
Spherica® products are particles consisting of chosen core material based on applications that is plated with various metal coating. The unique of the Spherica technology is to provide highly uniform and controlled particles in defined sizes, and with even and uniform metal plating to the particle surface. A wide selection of Silver, Nickel and Gold coated products are commercially available coated on a variety of core particles sizes between 3µm and 40µm. Every product is designed to meet the wide applications demands required by the conductive markets and our customers.
