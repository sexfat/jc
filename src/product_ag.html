Monosized metal-coated polymer particles

Spherica™ Ag

Spherica™ Ag particle is the next generation filler for isotropic conductive adhesives(ICA), designed for microelectronics interconnect, die attach and solar module manufacturing. This silver coated polymer particles with much lower silver consumption than traditional silver flake fillers, provide low resistivity in performance while cutting costs. Spherica™ Ag particles is thermally compatible with adhesive resins which improve joint reliability compared to silver flake fillers. Furthermore, the spherical shape enables jet-printing by avoiding nozzle clogging issues.

Product features

  • Truly spherical particles with highly uniformed size for jetting applications.
  • Cost efficient – up to 90% silver reduction
  • Create higher stand-off adhesive
  • Superior reliablility
